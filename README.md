# Mobile-Sales-Analysis-

🔍 Objective:
The goal was to design an interactive dashboard to analyze mobile sales across dimensions like time, location, brand, and payment methods. It helps stakeholders track trends, identify top products, and make data-driven decisions.

🔄 Process:

KPI Development:
Developed key metrics such as Total Sales (769M), Total Quantity (19K), Total Transactions (4K), and Average Price (40K). Used DAX measures for dynamic calculations and filtering across Month, Brand, and Payment Method.

Data Analysis & Modeling:
Performed data cleaning using Power Query. Built table relationships and created calculated columns and measures to support dynamic slicing and drill-down.

Visualizations:

Line Chart: Monthly sales trend showing fluctuations in quantity sold throughout the year.
Map Visual: City-wise sales distribution across India to identify high-performing regions.
Bar Chart: Sales comparison across top mobile models (iPhone SE, OnePlus Nord, Galaxy Note 20).
Pie Chart: Sales distribution by payment methods (UPI, Debit Card, Credit Card, Cash).
Table: Brand-wise total sales and quantity contribution.
Customer Ratings Funnel: Breakdown of customer satisfaction levels.
Area Chart: Sales trend by day of the week.
Slicers: Interactive filters for Month, Mobile Model, Brand, and Payment Method.

💡 Insights:

Peak sales observed in March and July, indicating seasonal demand trends.
UPI and Card payments contribute the majority of transactions, showing digital preference.
Apple and Samsung lead in total sales, while other brands follow closely.
Major sales concentration in metro cities like Delhi, Mumbai, and Bangalore.
Customer ratings are mostly 4 and 5, indicating high satisfaction levels.
Sales are highest on weekends, especially Saturday.

📊 Conclusion:
The dashboard delivers a complete view of sales performance using Power BI, DAX, and data modeling, helping drive actionable insights and better decision-making.
